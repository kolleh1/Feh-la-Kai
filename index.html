Class President

Biography: My name is John Prosper Gibson. I'm a promising entrepreneur/ Youth leader and a Youth and women Activist who has made a significant impact in the youth community of Liberia. Born on October 15 2005 , in Liberia, Prosper grew up with a passion for innovation and a drive to succeed. After completing his secondary education at Effort Baptist Church School, Prosper is also one of the founder of the Youth Congress Association International Liberia. It's one of the biggest youth organizations currently in Liberia. His hard work and dedication over the years have gained him the position to serve as the Executive Director for YCAIL , and over the years, Prosper has worked with other Youth organizations in and across Liberia. including a sustainable technology company and a non-profit organization dedicated to education and healthcare. Throughout his career, Prosper has received numerous awards and recognition for his entrepreneurial spirit and commitment to social responsibility. He has been featured in several youth programs, including Entrepreneur, Skill training and has spoken at conferences around Liberia. Despite his many accomplishments, Prosper remains humble and dedicated to giving back to his community. He is a devoted Christian and also works in the church and enjoys spending time outdoors and practicing soccer in his free time. Prosper Gibson is a passionate and dedicated student leader serving as the President of the Feh La Kai Class at Effort Baptist Church School. Known for his exemplary academic performance and strong leadership skills, Prosper has consistently been a role model to his peers. With a vision to inspire and empower his fellow students, Prosper actively organizes initiatives that promote academic excellence and community engagement. He is also a member of the school’s debate team and a volunteer for local outreach programs. His commitment to education and service reflects the core values of Effort Baptist Church School. Prosper believes in the motto "Leaders of Tomorrow," striving to create opportunities for his classmates to thrive and achieve their full potential.

Speech:Good day, esteemed teachers, fellow students, and honored guests, It is with great humility and pride that I stand before you as the President of the Feh La Kai Class, the Leaders of Tomorrow. Together, we represent the bright future of Effort Baptist Church School, a place that has not only nurtured our minds but also shaped our character. As we journey through this academic year, I encourage each of us to embrace unity, diligence, and a shared vision for success. Let us support one another in academics, personal growth, and service to our community. Together, we can overcome any challenge and achieve greatness. Thank you for entrusting me with this responsibility. I promise to lead with integrity and work tirelessly to make our class and school proud. Let us rise as the true leaders of tomorrow! Thank you, and may God bless us all.

Class Vice President

Biography:Kermie Sulon is a driven and compassionate leader, proudly serving as the Vice President of the Feh La Kai Class at Effort Baptist Church School. Known for his teamwork and problem-solving abilities, Kermie is dedicated to fostering a spirit of unity and collaboration among his peers. He actively participates in school initiatives, combining his leadership role with a strong commitment to academic excellence and extracurricular involvement. Kermie’s vision is to ensure that every student in the class feels supported and empowered to reach their full potential. With a deep sense of responsibility and service, Kermie Sulon continues to inspire his classmates as a true leader of tomorrow.

Speech:Good day, teachers, students, and distinguished guests, It is an honor to serve as the Vice President of the Feh La Kai Class, the Leaders of Tomorrow. Together with our President, Prosper Gibson, and our incredible classmates, I am committed to building a united and vibrant class community. As we navigate the challenges and opportunities of this academic year, I encourage each of us to bring our best selves to the table—whether in the classroom, on the field, or in service to others. Our strength lies in our ability to work together, support one another, and aim for excellence in everything we do. I am here to listen, to act, and to serve, ensuring that every voice in our class is heard and valued. Together, we will leave a legacy of determination and achievement for future leaders to follow. Thank you for your trust and support. Let’s make this year unforgettable! May God bless us all.
